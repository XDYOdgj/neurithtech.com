Elegant 12x16 Collage Frame with Real Glass and White Mat

Bring style and sentiment to your home with this beautiful collage frame designed for showcasing cherished memories, family art, or collection cards. The high-quality white mat fits up to 13 photos, allowing for flexible and creative display options whether you prefer a grid or a single focal piece.

Crafted from solid black wood, this frame features a 0.75'' wide border and thick 0.85''. molding, offering both durability and a refined appearance. The real glass ensures your photos remain safe and visually striking, making it a perfect addition to any interior space that values both aesthetics and functionality.

With three hangers included, this frame can be easily mounted on walls or tabletops, allowing for versatile placement. This is an ideal choice for framing a single 12x16 photo without the mat, perfect for those looking for a minimalist yet modern design. Whether used as a gift or a decorative piece, this collage frame combines practicality with charm for a lasting display.

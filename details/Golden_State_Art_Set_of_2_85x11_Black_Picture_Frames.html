Elevate Your Space with the Golden State Art Set of 2 Black Wooden Picture Frames

Add a touch of elegance and modern style to your home or office with this sleek set of two black wooden picture frames. Designed for versatility, each frame features a black mat that perfectly fits a 5x7 photo with an opening size of 4.5x6.5 inches. The durable real glass cover ensures your images are displayed in sharp, clear detail, while the D-Ring hangers make wall mounting or hanging on a display stand effortless.

Whether you're framing cherished memories, professional artwork, or important documents, these frames blend functionality with refined aesthetics. The swivel tabs allow for easy rotation, letting you switch between portrait and landscape views with just a simple turn. This set is ideal for display on a tabletop with the included easel stand or as a striking wall feature.

T10 Wedge Base LED Bulb

Upgrade your lighting with the Bayshe T10 wedge base LED bulb. Designed for low-voltage applications, these bulbs offer a powerful 500 lumens of light with a 3Watt equivalent to a traditional 30W halogen. They're energy-efficient, producing significantly less power while delivering bright, consistent illumination. With an impressive 35000-hour lifespan, they reduce the need for frequent replacements and save you money in the long run.

Built with a durable ceramic and clear PC material, these bulbs are safer, cooler, and more shatter-resistant than standard halogen models. The warm 2700K light creates a cozy and inviting atmosphere, perfect for both indoor and outdoor settings. Ideal for use in campers, RVs, motorhomes, and boats, these bulbs are also great for outdoor path lighting, landscape gardens, and patios.

The plug-and-play design makes installation a breeze, eliminating the need for extra wiring or adapters. Enjoy reliable performance with 360-degree beam coverage, ensuring every corner of your workspace or living area is well-lit. Whether you're illuminating your campsite, shop, or backyard, these bulbs are a practical, durable, and efficient lighting solution.
